Welcome

The Bazaar is fanmade site for the players of Conquerors Blade. Point of the site is to make the world of Concuerors Blade slightly smaller for new players. Houses play a big role to making the game experience good and there is no ingame solution for players to find the _right_ house for them. I hope this site will serve you well and you will find the house where you can grow. Write your application to the house in our discord. I hope to see you on the battlefield!

Dear house leader

Bazaar tryes to offer you the best possible service to make your house prosper. We welcome you to to the bazaar and may the fortune favor your house!
